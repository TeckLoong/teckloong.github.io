About Myself

I enjoy the accompany of my friends and goes around with the people wherever they go.

I recently changed as I am not having a balanced time management between studies and relax.

I took this opportunity to go on a vacation and enjoy myself.

I enjoyed the 7 days and 6 nights going out on a trip to Tanjong Balai and have taken many photos there.

I could not relax myself hence instead of going home to spend my holidays, I came here to play.

I enjoyed myself there as I have been away from country.

Day 1

On the first day, I went to rest at home and checked in to the house.

It was a tiring and long day.

We sat in the SINDO ferry trip to Tanjung Balai and it was 1 and a 1/2 hours long.

Day 2

On second day, I went to travel around Meral, a state of Balai, and had enjoyed the place outside your house.

I had the chance to learn more about the culture, the livelihood and the environment of the place.

It was an eye-opener and renewing for me to stay there for a long time.

Day 3

On the third day, I went to play snorkelling at the place outside Naga Mart with my family.

Also went to play the arcade games and eat the fried chicken with drink.

I felt very relieved and relaxed not to think about the assignments and homework I had.

Day 4

On the fourth day, we went to go eat and play badminton on our way in the van.

It was raining heavily on that day but I could not manage to take any photos as it was raining heavily on our way there.

But I took some time off my work to play to unwind myself.

I felt very satisfied and excited to play in the indoor sport hall.

Day 5

On fifth day, we went to sing the Karoke and sang many songs.

At night we went to play the games carnival with many games station but I rode the motocycle.

It was very nerve-wracking at the start of ride.

But I could not overcome it and so eventually I paced myself to ride and able to ride again.

Day 6

On the sixth day, we went to the beach and played the swing and swim in the sea.

We built sand pillow, covered myself with sand and made a sand hole for umbrella to shade from the sun.

I felt therapeutic and I love the feeling of lying down on the sand and face the hot sun.

Day 7

On the seventh day I went to the Pasar Mart to shop for the daily groceries and supplies.

I felt like I am refreshed and renewed that I manage to take a walk outside the pasar mart.
